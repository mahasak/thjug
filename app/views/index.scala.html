THJUG is a Java & JVM user group held in Bangkok focusing on the JVM technology stack. Java & JVM is an Open Source technology that gained a lot of popularity since 1999, developed by James Gosling, Mike Sheridan, and Patrick Naughton in Sun Microsystems

This technology is used by many of the biggest companies in the world because of its proven ability to enable better quality software. Want to learn the tech? Start here! We are very friendly and we help beginners get started and CTOs to get smarter. That's how the THJUG community works!

SPONSORS



If you guys can help us out a little bit, it would mean the world to us. We are literally paying out of our own pockets now to help grow the community in Thailand, and we can't do these events much longer on our own as they continue to grow so fast.

The tech scene in Bangkok is SCREAMING for developers and we drag them out from universities and every other cave they live in to get them to open their eyes and see how much the world has to offer them.

Just try to imagine all the things you take for granted that they might not even have heard about. That thing might be YOUR service!

Most people have heard about "Cloud", "Big Data", and how important metrics are, but have they tried Appsignal, Mixpanel, intercom.io, or know about Microsoft's BizSpark program?

This is YOUR chance to reach the community in a city of 15 million people—with just a few hundred bucks you can increase your client base in a place you hadn't even thought about yesterday! Please help us help you by raising awareness of your products. This is how we all thrive together!
